🌹 Poem 1

Shadowed heart, a silent cry,
Held by love I can’t deny.
Across the class, I watched you shine,
Hope so cruel—never mine.
Every word, a bittersweet song,
Echoed in me, though you moved on.
Never yours, yet forever lost,
A love unspoken, a heart that’s crossed.

💖 Poem 2

Flowers bloom when you pass by,
A glow like stars lights up the sky.
Time slows down when you're near,
I lose myself, my world is clear.
My heart beats just for you, so true,
A dream I wish would one day come true.

✨ Poem 3 T/h2>

Fading in your love, yet you never see my pain.
All I wish for is your hand, but my hopes fall like rain.
Remember me or forget me, my heart still beats your name.
Every glance of yours burns me, yet I smile the same.
Holding onto dreams that will never come true.
All I ever wanted was to be loved by you.

💔 Poem 4 Arabic

Ḥubbuki badā hamsan, aṣbaḥa qadara,
Mithlu al-shamsi fī samāʾī ẓahara.
Yaskunu ṭayfuki fī ʿuyūnī,
Rūḥī tahwāki ka-junūnī.
Aḥlāmī an akūna maʿaki,
ʾuḥibbuki dawmān, wa lan ansāki.

🌙 Poem 5

Sold your soul for fleeting highs,
A new boy every day—no shame, no ties.
Never faithful, never true,
In every bed, yet none want you.
Your love is cheap, your touch unclean,
A name now lost, nowhere to be seen.

🔥 Poem 6

Zero shame, just games you play,
One more boy thrown away.
Your love is fake, your touch untrue,
A new man every night—who even wants you?
