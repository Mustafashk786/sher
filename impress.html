Impress Your Love 💕

1. Teri chaahat mein aisi shifa si hai,
Jo zakhm bhi de, par dawa si hai.
Tera saath ho toh har raat chandni,
Tere bina zindagi bas saza si hai.

2. Teri zulfon ki chhaya mein jeena hai mujhe,
Teri aankhon ke jaam se peena hai mujhe.
Mohabbat ka izhaar lafzon se kyun karun,
Bas teri baahon mein khona hai mujhe.

3. Tera naam likhun toh har harf khushbu lutaaye,=
Teri yaad aaye toh hawayein naghme / geet sunaaye.
Meri har baat mein sirf tera zikr basa rahe,
Jaise chandni raat mein chand noor barsaaye.

Teri hansi meri subah ka ujala ban jaaye,
Teri baatein rooh ko choo kar dua ban jaaye.
Mujh mein tu hai, yeh har saans gawahi de,
Teri mohabbat meri rooh ka sukoon ban jaaye.
